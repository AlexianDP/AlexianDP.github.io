Updated computers for library users

VILNIUS TECH Library provides high quality information and services for study and research process, replenishes the fund of information resources with printed and electronic publications, updates and develops the computer infrastructure.

This year, for your convenience, we have updated the stationary computers in the Customer Service Department (Saulėtekio al. 14, hall 101), which are equipped with the most necessary software for studies and science (Autodesk, AutoCard, SOLIDWORKS, WaterGEMS, SewerCAD, MATLAB, etc.). You can find more software for studies and science at https://studsoft.vilniustech.lt. To use the computers, log in with your institutional login data.
